App Features

Easy Registration

Register quickly with your Aadhaar card and complete the application process in minutes.

Biometric Verification

Secure authentication using biometric verification to prevent fraud and ensure benefits reach the right beneficiaries.

Real-time Updates

Track your application status and receive real-time notifications about payment disbursements.

Document Scanner

Built-in document scanner for easy upload of required documents without the need for separate scanning apps.

Direct Benefit Transfer

Receive payments directly to your bank account and view transaction history within the app.

Multilingual Support

Use the app in Hindi, English, and other local languages for better accessibility across Jharkhand.
